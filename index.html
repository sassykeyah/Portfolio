I believe good design starts by listening, that design is a conversation. With a BDes in Interaction Design and strong interest in designing for accessibility, I create digital experiences that are clear, usable and are inclusive for all.

24hr Digital Design Competition - '404: Page Not Found'

Lost in a world of 404, you must find the 302. 404NotFound explores digital disconnection as a metaphor for rediscovery. Created for the UC 24hr Design Challenge, it navigates the prompt of "lost and found". It is structured as an interactive narrative, with the user clicking and scrolling to reveal information.

Spotify Rewind

A trip through Australia's most significant historical moments, and the most popular songs from that year. From the 1956 Melbourne Olympics to the 2023 Voice to Parliament referendum, discover how our nations story can be emulated through the connection of music and history.
